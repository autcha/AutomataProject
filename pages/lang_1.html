Automata & DNA Computing

The Ben Shapiro engine is a two state finate state machine. The automaton accepts x in {a, b}*. There are 8 possible encoding of a and b and the 8 possible transition rules are predetermined sequences of DNA. The DNA computer relies on 2 enzymes: Ligase and FokI. The first is responsible for movinf to the input word and the second to ligate (split) the DNA.

The alphabet is {a, b}* and accepted strings are in the form (b + ba)*

Symbols

$$ a \rightarrow TGGCT \\ b \rightarrow GCAGG \\ \langle S_{0} , a \rangle \rightarrow GGCT \\ \langle S_{0} , b \rangle \rightarrow CAGG \\ \langle S_{1} , a \rangle \rightarrow TGGC\\ \langle S_{1}, b \rangle \rightarrow GCAG $$

Terminator

$$ t \rightarrow GTCGG \\ \langle S_{0} , t \rangle \rightarrow TCGG \\ \langle S_{1} , t \rangle \rightarrow GTCG $$
