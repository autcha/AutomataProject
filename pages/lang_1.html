Automata & DNA Computing

The Ben Shapiro engine is a two state finate state machine. The automaton accepts x in {a, b}*. The possible encoding of a and b are predetermined as so are the 8 possible transition rules between two states in a finite state machine. The DNA computer relies on 2 enzymes: Ligase and FokI. The first is responsible for movinf to the input word and the second to ligate (split) the DNA.
